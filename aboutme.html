About Me

Hello! My name is Raquel Rubio, and I am a creative, communicative, curious and empathetic person, passionate about design as well as psychology and human behavior. After more than 5 years of experience in Graphic Design and Photography, I decided to study UX/UI Design at Ironhack. This change was given by the necessity to put people back at the center of my work, because this had evolved to a point where I was creating graphics non-stop, without thinking about the user or a real utility. I've found myself working with no other purpose than to reach a maximum number of weekly compositions. Fortunately, now I am rebuilding myself and I have changed the empty and repeatable campaigns for: product design, user experience, UX research, usability, interactions, wireframes or prototypes, and adapted them to the users and their way of life, the world and the last technologies. In the future I would like to find a multidisciplinary company that is ethical with its products and work methods, that values ​​teamwork and good labor relations. I am passionate about my work, and I'm looking for a job where get involved and grow with each project. Now you know me a little better, do you want to talk? I will be happy to know each other better.
